BRANDING PROJECT

Mustache Cafe

Branding project includes logo, poster, flyer, royalty card and web design. The idea of the coffee shop is “MUSTACHE CAFE” which offers organic coffee and also retro modern atmosphere. Design is simple and flat but also using subtle grain texture can broaden the range of expression. To make it friendly design and to avoid being a just macho design, there are rounded corner strokes and feminine patterns and elements.
