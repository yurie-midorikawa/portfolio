EXHIBITION

Bubbles

2012 exhibition that is held with my university colleagues called ‘BUNKAI KUMITATE’ in Japan. We decided on the mutual theme and created different work pieces individually. The theme was to create the art piece that you can easily dissemble and assemble at the same time. Based on this theme, I designed and created the silver accessory that you can customize to make the different length and volume of the accessory by connecting/separating silver rings. I also draw the bubble poster using Photoshop. I was also in charge of creating the exhibition poster and website.
