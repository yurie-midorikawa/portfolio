APP ICON/LOGO DESIGN

App icon and logo of the dating app in Japan. By adding a fork, which represent the food inside of the heart successfully conveys that the purpose of this app are both finding a new person and having a nice dinner. Using orange color is also effective especially when the target users are both male and female.
